About Eric Ries...

The head in a whole movement, Erick Ries has redefined (at least for me) the way to entrepeneurship

While at Yale, he was co-founder of Catalyst Recruiting, an online forum for university students to network with potential employers. He took a leave of absence to pursue Catalyst Recruiting, but the company soon failed.

Eric used failure, as the only thing it should be used for, to learn from his mistakes and build resilience. Ahead of his carrer laid importatn opportunities to seize.

Eric went back to college with all the experience early entrepeneurship taught him. This became the stepping stone of the tech-driven entrepeneur.
